SEC 111 Applied Cryptography

This course builds upon the SEC 110 course and covers the use of common cryptography applications, utilities and protocols such as TLS/SSL, HTTPS, OpenSSL and GNU Privacy Guard (GPG). It also covers certificates and the public key infrastructure (PKI).
